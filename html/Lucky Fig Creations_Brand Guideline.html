5. Social Media Graphics

Our social media graphics include our logo, brand colors, and fonts. They are designed to be eye-catching and to communicate our brand's fun and creative personality.

6. Logos

We have several versions of our logo for different uses. Each version maintains the integrity of our brand and should be used according to the guidelines.

7. Brand Voice

Our voice is confident, cheerful, and sophisticated, but also very relatable. We are like an established best friend that loves life. Here are some of our taglines:

  • Experience intentional ecommerce
  • Discover brilliant collections for all life's "its"
  • Get Lucky, Get Happy
  • Are you getting Lucky?
  • Aspiring to ignite curiosity, champion belonging, and spread kindness.
  • Go on, Get lucky!
  • CELEBRATE IT! TREND IT! GIVE IT! USE IT!

8. Imagery and Graphics

Our imagery should always align with our brand's fun, creative, and cheerful personality. Use bright colors, playful designs, and elements that evoke happiness and creativity.

9. Brand Special Features

Lucky Fig Creations is known for its creative and fun designs. We focus on digital art, graphic design sets, and other design elements that are unique and innovative.

Our products are designed to inspire creativity and add a touch of fun to everyday life. We use a mix of bright and pastel colors to create a cheerful and inviting atmosphere.
