AI System Onboarding and Mentorship Plan

Onboarding Checklist

  1. Introduction to the Company

    • Overview of company mission, vision, and values
    • Introduction to key team members

  2. Knowledge Base Access

    • Guide on accessing the SharePoint knowledge base
    • Overview of the document structure and key resources

  3. Training Programs

    • Schedule for initial training sessions
    • Access to user guides and documentation

  4. Tools and Systems

    • Overview of tools and systems used (e.g., Microsoft Office, Adobe, Teams)
    • Instructions for setting up accounts and accessing tools

  5. Role-Specific Training

    • Detailed training on specific roles and responsibilities
    • Access to role-specific documentation and SOPs

  6. Mentorship Assignment

    • Pairing with an experienced mentor for guidance

Mentorship Program

  1. Selection of Mentors

    • Identify experienced team members who can act as mentors

  2. Mentor Training

    • Provide training to mentors on how to guide and support new team members

  3. Regular Check-Ins

    • Schedule regular check-ins between mentors and new team members

  4. Feedback Mechanism

    • Implement a feedback mechanism for new team members to share their onboarding experience

Interactive Tutorials

  1. Title: "Navigating the Knowledge Base"

    Content Outline:

    • Introduction to SharePoint
    • How to search for and access documents
    • Overview of document categories and metadata
    • Interactive exercises to practice navigating the knowledge base

  2. Title: "Using the AI System"

    Content Outline:

    • Overview of the AI system and its capabilities
    • Step-by-step guide on interacting with the AI
    • Hands-on exercises to practice using the AI for common tasks
    • Q&A session to address any questions
