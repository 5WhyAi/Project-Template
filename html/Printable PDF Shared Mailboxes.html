Adding Shared Inboxes to Outlook

Web Outlook:

  1. Log in to outlook.office.com
  2. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Open another mailbox"
  4. Enter the shared inbox email address
  5. Click "Open"
  6. The shared inbox will appear in your folder list

Desktop Outlook:

  1. Open Outlook desktop app
  2. Click "File" > "Account Settings" > "Account Settings"
  3. In the "Email" tab, click "New..."
  4. Enter your name and the shared inbox email address
  5. Click "Next" and follow the prompts to complete setup
  6. The shared inbox will appear as a separate account

Note: You may need admin rights to access shared inboxes. Contact IT support if you encounter issues.
