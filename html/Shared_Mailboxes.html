Quick Guide: Adding Shared Inboxes and Aliases in Outlook

Web:

  1. Log in to outlook.office.com
  2. Click profile picture > "Open another mailbox..." > Enter shared inbox email
  3. Compose new email > Click "More actions" > Show From
  4. Click From > Other Email Address... > Enter alias
  5. Compose and send

Desktop:

  1. File > Account Settings > New > Enter shared inbox email
  2. New Email > Options tab > From button
  3. Click From > Other E-mail Address... > Enter alias
  4. Compose and send
