1. Introduction

Brand Name: Lucky Fig

Type: LLC, Partnership

Mission:

Lucky Fig's mission is to compile and curate innovative, on-trend lifestyle products, offerings, and services that embrace the unique human experience while fostering unity, acceptance, and empathy for all.

To inspire a movement of positive change and acceptance of all, leading by example as pioneers in emotionally intelligent, human-experience-driven commerce.
