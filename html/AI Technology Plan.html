AI TECHNOLOGY PLAN

Executive Summary

Our AI Technology Plan centers around a Core AI system that integrates with our operating companies to drive efficiency, innovation, and growth. Key components include:

  • A centralized Core AI with API access to global and OpCo-specific SharePoints
  • Company Assistant GPTs serving three primary functions:
    • Product Support Bot for customer interactions
    • Brand and Commercial Executive for content and marketing
    • Customizable product offering (e.g., 5WHY STRATEGIES Power 360 Bot)
  • Comprehensive capabilities spanning from operational efficiency to advanced marketing and networking

This structure enables us to leverage AI across our organization, enhancing customer support, brand management, and creating new revenue streams through AI-as-a-product offerings.

Core AI System Overview

The Core AI system is linked to each operating company and has the following access:

  • API access to L&J Global Solutions SharePoint (houses overarching documents for Operating Companies)
  • API access to each OpCo SharePoint

This access allows the Core AI to:

  • Discern efficiency opportunities across OpCos
  • Identify opportunities for improvement
  • Find financial savings
  • Optimize purchasing and manufacturing efficiencies
  • Facilitate digital product cross-selling
  • Enhance general cross-selling
  • Improve advertising strategies

OpCo Knowledge Bases and Company Assistant GPTs

The OpCo Knowledge Bases feed the Company Assistant GPTs in three main ways:

1. Product Support Bot

  • Interacts with customers
  • Provides customer support
  • Answers FAQs
  • Assists with troubleshooting

2. Brand and Commercial Executive

  • Generates content in the brand voice
  • Identifies chat sections for social media/advertising
  • Creates content for various platforms (Google Ads, TikTok, Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram, Snap, Meta, LinkedIn)
  • Finds media exposure and partnership opportunities
  • Triggers intro emails via LinkedIn or Outlook
  • Identifies potential LinkedIn connections

3. Product Offering (e.g., 5WHY STRATEGIES Power 360 Bot)

  • Offers different access levels for training the GPT using standard documents
  • Highest level access creates a CoreGPT
