5WHY Global Strategies

Uncovering Root Causes, Delivering Global Solutions

Brand Overview

Mission: To empower organizations worldwide with innovative problem-solving methodologies, driving continuous improvement and sustainable growth.

Vision: To be the global leader in strategic problem-solving, known for transforming challenges into opportunities across industries and cultures.

Values: Curiosity, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Excellence

Brand Voice

5WHY Global Strategies communicates with a voice that is:

  • Authoritative yet approachable
  • Analytical and insightful
  • Solution-oriented
  • Globally minded

Key Messages

  • Uncover the root cause to solve complex problems
  • Global expertise, local understanding
  • Driving continuous improvement through strategic analysis

Logo Usage

The 5WHY Global Strategies logo should be used consistently to maintain brand recognition:

  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "5" in the logo
  • Minimum size: 1.5 inches / 38mm in print, 150 pixels on screen
  • Use the full-color version on light backgrounds, and the white version on dark backgrounds
  • Avoid stretching, distorting, or changing the logo's colors

Imagery

5WHY Global Strategies imagery should reflect our global reach and problem-solving focus:

  • Use images that represent diverse cultures and industries
  • Include visuals of teams collaborating and problem-solving
  • Incorporate abstract representations of the 5 WHY methodology
  • Opt for clean, modern imagery with a professional feel

Brand Applications

Presentations

Use clean, minimalist slides with ample white space. Incorporate infographics and data visualizations using the brand color palette.

Website

Design a user-friendly interface with clear navigation. Use case studies and interactive elements to demonstrate the 5WHY methodology.

Marketing Materials

Create cohesive brochures, white papers, and digital assets that showcase global expertise and client success stories.
