Brand Overview

Mission: To bring luck and prosperity to our customers through unique fig-inspired creations.

Vision: To be the world's leading provider of fig-themed products, known for quality, creativity, and positive energy.

Values: Creativity, Quality, Positivity, Sustainability, Customer Delight

Brand Voice

Lucky Fig Creations speaks with a voice that is:

  • Optimistic and uplifting
  • Friendly and approachable
  • Clever and witty
  • Passionate about figs and good fortune

Key Messages

  • Every fig product brings a touch of luck to your life
  • Handcrafted with care and positive energy
  • Unique designs that celebrate the beauty of figs

Logo Usage

The Lucky Fig Creations logo should always be used in a way that maintains its integrity and visibility:

  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "L" in "Lucky"
  • Minimum size: 1 inch / 25mm in print, 100 pixels on screen
  • Use the full-color version on light backgrounds, and the white version on dark backgrounds

Imagery

Lucky Fig Creations imagery should reflect our brand's positive and creative spirit:

  • Use bright, natural lighting
  • Feature figs prominently, but creatively
  • Include diverse, happy people interacting with our products
  • Incorporate elements of nature and good fortune symbols

Brand Applications

Product Packaging

Use eco-friendly materials with our primary color palette. Include our logo and a short, lucky message on each package.

Website

Maintain a clean, easy-to-navigate design with plenty of white space. Use high-quality product images and incorporate fig-inspired decorative elements.

Social Media

Share behind-the-scenes content, customer testimonials, and "lucky moments" featuring our products. Use our brand colors in graphics and maintain a consistent, positive tone in all posts.
