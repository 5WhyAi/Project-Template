Organizational Strategy

Adoption Strategy

  • Leadership Buy-In

    Ensure leadership supports and promotes the use of the AI system.

  • Incentives

    Offer incentives for employees who actively use and contribute to the AI system.

  • User Support

    Provide ongoing support to help users overcome any challenges.

Hiring Guide

  • Start with L&J Global Solutions

    Focus on hiring key leadership and support roles at the holding company level to ensure effective oversight and management of all OpCos.

Hiring Plan

  1. Step 1: Leadership and Support Roles for L&J Global Solutions

    Hire key leadership and support roles.

  2. Step 2: Critical Roles for OpCos

    Hire critical roles for each OpCo to ensure smooth operations and growth.

Characteristics for Leaders

  • Strategic Thinking: Ability to think strategically and align departmental goals with overall business objectives.
  • Leadership Skills: Proven leadership skills with experience in managing teams.
  • Adaptability: Ability to adapt to changing environments and drive innovation.
  • Communication Skills: Excellent communication skills to effectively convey ideas and strategies.

Characteristics for Individual Contributors

  • Technical Proficiency: Strong technical skills relevant to the role.
  • Problem-Solving: Ability to solve problems effectively and efficiently.
  • Collaboration: Ability to work well in teams and collaborate with others.
  • Attention to Detail: High level of attention to detail and accuracy.
