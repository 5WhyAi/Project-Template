AI Technology Plan

Overview

Our AI Technology Plan is designed to leverage artificial intelligence across our organization, enhancing customer support, brand management, and creating new revenue streams through AI-as-a-product offerings.

Key Components

  • Centralized Core AI System
  • Company Assistant GPTs
  • SharePoint Integration

Core AI System

The Core AI system is the central hub of our AI infrastructure, with the following capabilities:

  • API access to L&J Global Solutions SharePoint
  • API access to each OpCo SharePoint
  • Cross-company efficiency analysis
  • Financial optimization
  • Enhanced cross-selling capabilities

Company Assistant GPTs

Our AI assistants serve three primary functions:

1. Product Support Bot

  • Customer interaction
  • FAQ management
  • Troubleshooting assistance

2. Brand and Commercial Executive

  • Content generation in brand voice
  • Multi-platform marketing content creation
  • Partnership opportunity identification
  • Networking and outreach automation

3. Customizable Product Offering

  • Tiered access levels for GPT training
  • Example: 5WHY STRATEGIES Power 360 Bot

Implementation

Our AI Technology Plan is implemented through:

  • Integration with existing SharePoint infrastructure
  • Development of specialized GPTs for each function
  • Continuous learning and optimization of AI models
  • Regular updates to knowledge bases and training data

Benefits

  • Improved operational efficiency
  • Enhanced customer experience
  • Data-driven decision making
  • New revenue streams through AI products
  • Competitive advantage in the market
